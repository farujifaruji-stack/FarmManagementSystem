# EPIC-004 – Financial Management & Digital Receipts

## Overview

The **Financial Management & Digital Receipts** module provides a centralized solution for managing the farm's financial operations. It enables administrators to record income and expenses, manage pricing information, organize digital receipts, and maintain accurate financial records for reporting, auditing, and operational decision-making.

---

## Business Goal

Provide a reliable and centralized financial management system that improves record keeping, simplifies expense tracking, and supports digital documentation across farm operations.

---

## Description

This module allows administrators to:

- Record and manage farm income.
- Record and manage farm expenses.
- Organize digital receipts.
- Manage receipt payments.
- Manage receipt items.
- Maintain income and expense categories.
- Configure product pricing.
- Support financial reporting and auditing.
